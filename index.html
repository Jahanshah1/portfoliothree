Journey into the World of Development:

Hello! I embarked on my coding adventure in 2020, diving into a diverse tech pool encompassing AI, frontend, backend, and APIs. My fascination with technology led me to the dynamic realm of Web3, specifically within the Ethereum ecosystem.

Venture into Web3 Governance with Synkarnia:

In early 2022, I co-founded Synkarnia, a Web3 governance token initiative aimed at fostering a community dedicated to sustainable development. We generated significant buzz and secured funding of ₹2 lakh at a valuation of ₹4 crore from an Indian business incubator. Despite regulatory challenges leading to the project's closure at the end of 2022, the experience was invaluable.

Synapsed Infotech Pvt Ltd - Bridging AI and Software:

In October 2022, I founded Synapsed Infotech Pvt Ltd, an AI-focused software startup. Our Proof of Concept garnered attention, featuring in the August 2023 edition of Business Connect magazine as one of the "Top 10 Most Promising Startups of 2023." As the core developer, I led the development of Project INTEGRA, an AI-based lead generation platform for Indian businesses, utilizing NLP for lead curation and AI-driven automated email campaigns.

Continuous Learning and Collaboration:

Continuing my journey, I actively participate in global tech events and hackathons, staying abreast of the latest technological advancements. I am dedicated to exploring innovative technologies like Zero Knowledge proofs, IPFS, and Large Language Models (LLMs), and fostering collaborative team environments.

Looking Ahead:

I'm excited to keep pushing the boundaries of technology, and I invite you to explore my portfolio to see the impact of my work. Let's connect and collaborate on future innovations!
